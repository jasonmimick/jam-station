How a drive works

You get in the car and either tap a saved channel URL in a radio app or open the station app and tell the AI "70s fusion, high energy." The brain tops up that channel's queue (Archive shows by rating, or your library for fusion), liquidsoap plays and crossfades it into the Icecast mount, and your phone streams it over Tailscale like any internet radio station — seamless on cellular. Spotify/YouTube channels don't touch the house at all: the AI maintains their playlists via API and the native apps play them in CarPlay.

Notes

Docker on the Mac mini runs fine via OrbStack, Colima, or Docker Desktop — everything here is network-only (no audio hardware needed on the server). Tailscale runs on the macOS host, not in a container; containers just publish ports on the host, and optionally tailscale serve can front them with HTTPS. Keep Icecast/Navidrome bound to the tailnet only — nothing needs to be exposed to the public internet. Start order: icecast → liquidsoap → radio-brain.
