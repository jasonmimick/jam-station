SESSION

Jason's radio station, on your Mac — live shows, his record shelf, always on the air.

Hi Dad — this little app tunes your Mac into my radio station. It plays live concert tapes all day (Grateful Dead, jazz, bluegrass…), and every CD I rip lands on its shelf. Nothing to maintain, nothing to pay for. Here's the whole setup — about two minutes.

1 · Install it

  1. Double-click the file I sent — Session-mac.zip. It unzips into an app called Session.
  2. Drag Session into your Applications folder.
  3. Double-click it to open. The Mac will refuse the first time — it says it can't verify the developer. That's expected (the developer is your son, and Apple wants $99 to vouch for me). Here's the handshake:
    1. Open → System Settings → Privacy & Security
    2. Scroll down — you'll see a note that "Session" was blocked
    3. Click Open Anyway, then confirm
    You only do this once. After that it opens like any app.

2 · Listen

  1. Session opens as a window, and it also lives in your menu bar — look for the little antenna up by the clock. Click either one.
  2. The left side lists the stations — each line shows what's playing on it right now. Click one. That's it — you're on the air.
  3. The controls sit along the bottom: play/pause, volume, and a red ON AIR light when you're live.
Closing the window doesn't stop the music — Session keeps playing from the menu bar. Click the antenna to get it back.

3 · The key (optional, but do it)

I sent you an email from the station with a passcode. In Session, click the ⚙ gear, type the passcode into the Members box, and press Enter. That unlocks the family side of the station:

  • The Shelf — my actual CD collection, ripped disc by disc. Click any record and it plays, cover art and all.
  • The genre stations — "From the Shelf — Jazz" and friends: pick one and it plays a fresh mix of my records in that style, endlessly.
  • Favourites — click the ♥ on anything you like and it's saved to your list.

If something acts up

  • Music stopped? It usually restarts itself within a few seconds. If not, click pause, then play.
  • Something looks stuck? Quit Session (click the antenna → the ⏻ button, or ⌘Q on the window) and open it again.
  • All else fails: the station also lives in any web browser at
    jam-station.runslab.run/dad — big buttons, one click, same music.
  • And you can always just call me.

The station broadcasts from the Mac mini in my house. When I rip a new CD, you'll see it appear on the Shelf a few minutes later — the collection literally grows while you listen. When I send you an updated app someday, the "Open Anyway" handshake happens once more; everything else stays put.
