Installing (30 seconds)

The app isn't in the App Store, so macOS asks once before it'll open. Here's the whole dance:

  1. Open the downloaded Session-mac.zip — it unzips to Session.app.
  2. Drag Session.app into your Applications folder.
  3. Right-click it → Open → Open again in the dialog. (Just this first time — after that it opens like anything else.)

The right-click-Open is macOS's way of letting you run an app from a small developer outside the App Store. You only do it once.
